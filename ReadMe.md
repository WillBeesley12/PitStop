PitStop
A voice-first, hands-free iOS app for discovering restaurants while you drive.
How it works
Tap once to start, then just talk. Say something like "find me a cheap taco place, I'm in a rush" or "somewhere nicer for dinner, I've got time." PitStop figures out what you want — cuisine, price, how much of a detour you'll tolerate — finds nearby restaurants on or near your route, and reads the top three options aloud with detour times. Pick one by voice, and you're on your way. No tapping, no scrolling, eyes on the road.
Overview
Searching for food on the road is dangerous and frustrating — existing apps assume you have your hands and eyes free, but drivers don't. PitStop solves this with a fully voice-driven flow: speak what you're craving, and the app finds nearby restaurants that require minimal route deviation, then reads the top options aloud with detour time so you can pick by voice without ever touching your phone.
